The short version

Customer success, learned from inside the chair.

I started in the operatory. As a Registered Dental Hygienist in high-volume Brooklyn and Staten Island practices, I learned exactly how a dental office runs — what makes a clinician trust a new tool, and what makes them quietly abandon it.

Then I took that fluency into the field. At Align Technology I converted 300+ practices to digital scanning workflows. At OraPharma (Bausch Health) I rank in the top 10 of ~70 reps nationally — growing my territory roughly 10% a quarter while protecting the accounts I've already won. And I founded GetDented to prove I can build a customer motion from a blank page.

The thread through all of it is the same: turn a signed contract into a practice that can't imagine working without you. That's customer success — and I've been doing it in the field for over a decade.
